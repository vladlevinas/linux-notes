# systemctl reboot

> Reboot the system.
> More information: <https://www.freedesktop.org/software/systemd/man/systemctl.html#reboot>.

- Reboot the system:

`systemctl reboot`

- Reboot into the BIOS/UEFI menu:

`systemctl reboot --firmware-setup`
